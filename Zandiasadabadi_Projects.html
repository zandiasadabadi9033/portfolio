Scratch Project

In High School, I took Computer Software Engeenering class and as a result of the class, I did my first ever programming project, on Scratch. In this project, I collaborated with my partner and we came up with "Back to School"; a game which is easy to play. All you have to do is to pay attention to the instructions, make the right decisions, and avoid getting into any problems that might stop you from going to school. This project gave me an idea about what to expect from the programming world.
